FAQ
Common questions
Do I need a website to use this?+
No. The assistant can be added to your Facebook page, Google Business profile, or we can create a simple standalone page for it.
What does "captures customer info" mean?+
When a customer chats, the assistant naturally collects their name and phone number or email. You get a summary so you can follow up directly.
How long does setup actually take?+
48 hours from when you send us your business info. Most clients are live the same day or next day.
What is the small monthly AI cost?+
The assistant runs on an AI platform that charges based on usage — typically $5 to $10 per month for a local business. This goes directly to the provider, not to us. We help you set it up.
What if a customer asks something the AI doesn't know?+
It lets them know it will pass the question along, collects their contact info, and you get notified. No customer gets left without a response.
What kinds of businesses is this for?+
Any local service business that gets messages or questions — barbershops, salons, HVAC, auto repair, dental, contractors, restaurants. If customers ask about your hours, prices, or availability, this is for you.
